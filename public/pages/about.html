Brainstorm: a personalized Webs of Trust Nostr Relay

Brainstorm calculates personalized trust scores based on input from your trusted nostr community. Personalized scores are calculated for the owner of the Brainstorm instance. Brainstorm also supports personalized trust score calculation for up to 10 friends (or customers, once that feature is implemented) of the owner (or more based on system specs).
Algorithms and scores include:
  • GrapeRank
  • PageRank
  • Hops
  • verified Follower Count
  • and more!
    • GrapeRank data sources:
  • Follows
  • Mutes
  • Reports
  • ... with more to come
    • These scores are made available to nostr clients using a variety of methods, most notably NIP-85: Trusted Assertions.
    Why personalized trust scores?
  • screen out spam
  • stratify content
  • content discovery
    • Brainstorm is currently in alpha and is not yet available for widespread use.
