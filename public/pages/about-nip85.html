About NIP-85

NIP-85 is a Nostr Improvement Proposal that defines a standard for publishing personalized trust scores in a way that is easily discoverable and verifiable by other users on the Nostr network.

What is NIP-85?

NIP-85 enables Nostr users to publish their personalized trust scores and rankings in a standardized format. This allows other applications and users to discover and utilize these trust metrics to enhance their Nostr experience.

Key Benefits for Developers:

  • Standardized Trust Data: Access consistent trust score formats across the network
  • Enhanced User Experience: Provide users with personalized content filtering and recommendations
  • Spam Prevention: Leverage community trust scores to reduce spam and low-quality content
  • Decentralized Reputation: Build reputation systems without centralized authorities
